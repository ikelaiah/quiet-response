Before you printFive quick checks for reliable results
  • Test with a real phone camera — especially before a big print run.
  • Keep the quiet zone at 4 or more for stickers and posters.
  • Dark code on a light background scans most reliably across devices.
  • Matte finishes scan better than glossy — glare is the enemy of little squares.
  • Printing small? Shorter links make coarser, easier-to-scan codes.
